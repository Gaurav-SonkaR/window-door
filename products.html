Windows enhanced with stability and durability

PlastOne is a leading profile in the industry of uPVC windows and doors in India. We offer profiles depending on modern and customized requirements. Our uPVC windows and doors are tested for physical and lead-free properties. Our brands of sliding windows and doors offer compatible, versatile and functional design keeping in mind the six climatic conditions prevalent in a region. They are divided on the bases of wind speed and our products are designed to take the wind load into consideration. Our mission is to provide superior service and unforgettable experiences for our customers.

uPVC Sliding windows

  • Choice of 3 sliding sashes in different heights and window size
  • Monorail type for economical version

  • Glass accommodation of 6&20mm thickness
